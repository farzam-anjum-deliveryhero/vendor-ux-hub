Team agreements
How we work together
🤝 Communication norms
Async-first. Respond to Slack within 24h on workdays. Use threads. Flag blockers in daily standup.
📐 Design quality bar
Always use Pando components. Document decisions in Figma comments before grooming.
🔁 Feedback culture
Feedback is about the work, not the person. Use "I noticed…" and "What if…" framing.
Design process
Four stages, from problem to handoff
1
Discovery & Problem framing
Define the problem with Product. Use the Problem Statement template in Figma before touching UI.
→ Open template
2
Research & Insights
Check existing research first. Log new sessions in the Research Hub before recruiting participants.
→ Research Hub
3
Design & Iterate
Use Pando component library. Share Figma link in Slack after each meaningful iteration for async feedback.
→ Figma Library
4
Review & Handoff
Run a critique session. Leave Figma comments for open decisions. Complete design spec before grooming.
→ Handoff checklist

Log new research
Follow these steps before starting any study
⚠️
Always register before you start. Log your study in the Research Tracker before recruiting. This prevents duplicate research across teams.
1
Create a research brief
Define your research questions, methodology, and timeline using the template.
→ Open Research Brief Template
2
Register in the Research Tracker
Add your study to the shared Notion database so the team can see what's in progress.
→ Open Research Tracker
3
Upload insights after completion
Share the final report using the Insight Report template and tag relevant stakeholders.
